About

I'm a Product Designer with experience designing solutions for complex enterprise domains in a cross-functional Agile setting.

I'm passionate about conducting user research and using research insights to drive the design of effective user experiences. By working with users directly, my goal is to create solutions that are readily adopted and save companies time, money, and resources on development.

I also have a diverse background of experience in software development, project management, quality assurance, and digital art, which all contribute to my success as a product designer and overall problem-solver.
